Overview of Ambulance Services

An ambulance service provides emergency medical assistance and patient transportation. It ensures timely response during medical crises, equipped with life-saving equipment and trained paramedics. The service includes rapid patient transport to hospitals, monitoring vitals, and providing immediate care, often critical for saving lives. Modern ambulance services often integrate GPS tracking, real-time communication, and medical data sharing with hospitals to improve efficiency and patient outcomes.
